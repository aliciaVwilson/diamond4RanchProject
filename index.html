ALLEN'S DIAMOND 4 RANCH

LANDER, WYOMING

We're located in the rugged Wind River Mountain Range and is Wyoming's highest wilderness guest ranch offering log cabins, mountain trail riding, fly fishing, wilderness pack trips, and guided hunting in Shoshone National Forest

Mountain Ranch Vacations

Join us off the grid in Wyoming's highest and most remote dude ranch. Rustic comfort, western hospitality, and a friendly staff make the Diamond 4 Ranch perfect for your summer vacation!

Wilderness Pack Trips

Our Guided Pack Trip allows you to enjoy riding, exploring, fly fishing or just relaxing while our guides prepare wonderful meals, tend camp and wrangle horses for you, deep in the heart of the Wind Rivers of Wyoming.

Guided Hunts

Allen's Diamond 4 Ranch is a family owned Wyoming Hunting Outfitter providing top quality Guided Hunts since 1973. Our friendly guides will assist you in a successful and memorable hunt for quality Pronghorn Antelope, Deer, Elk, Moose, and Bighorn Sheep in unspoiled country.

MOUNTAIN RANCH VACATIONS

RUSTIC COMFORT AND WESTERN HOSPITALITY

Experience our unique wilderness hospitality and the mountain grandeur that can only be enjoyed far away from noisy highways and crowded civilization. Each night you will be awed by the vast expanse of the milky way. With no distraction of town lights, the skies become a stargazers paradise. What better way to spend time and create lasting memories with your family?
